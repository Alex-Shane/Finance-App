What is Knight Investor?

Knight Investor is brought to you by undegraduate students with a vested interest in the financial industry. We belive all investors must start somewhere and that starting small is better than not starting at all! At its core, our website is a stepping stone for novice investors and a valuable tool for those interested in learning about the current state of the stock market and potentially finding undervalued comapanies. Our product provides statistical data on multiple stock exchanges and industries and runs numerous internal algorithms to generate our extensive reports. We hope you enjoy the product.
